# Client_Strategy_Brief_v2

## Project Summary
Signal over Noise needs a workshop-ready, mobile-first marketing site that converts interest into leads without feeling overbuilt. The site now has a defined two-step funnel: a low-friction workshop QR path that captures email via the Presenter Toolkit, and a higher-intent diagnostic path that leads into consults and paid services.

The site should feel premium, editorial, and credible: restrained overall, with selective visual drama. v1 should ship quickly, use placeholders only where necessary, and be easy for Ben to update in Pencil.

## Business Goal
Generate qualified leads for Signal over Noise, then move those leads toward:
- Slide Refinement
- Message Refinement
- Presentation Rehearsal
- Presentation Sprint
- Presentation Coaching
- Custom Workshops & Team Training

## Audience
### Primary audience for v1
Professionals presenting in English and multilingual settings.

### Expanded audience framing
Experts and teams presenting to international, multilingual, or mixed-language audiences.

### Audience positioning rule
Do not frame the audience as deficient or linguistically inadequate. Frame the problem around presentation stakes, clarity, structure, and delivery in multilingual communication contexts.

## Offer
### Primary workshop QR offer
**Signal over Noise Presenter Toolkit**

Toolkit assets:
- Script Development Worksheet
- Slide Review Checklist
- Presentation Rehearsal Plan

### Secondary higher-intent offer
**Free Presentation Diagnostic**
- Request-based, not the first ask for workshop QR visitors
- Includes a short PDF review before a short consult
- Diagnostic types:
  - Slide Deck Diagnostic
  - Script Diagnostic
  - Video Performance Diagnostic

## Primary CTA + Secondary CTA
### Site-wide default
- **Primary CTA:** Request a Free Presentation Diagnostic
- **Secondary CTA:** Get the Signal over Noise Presenter Toolkit

### Workshop QR funnel
- **Primary CTA on /scan:** Get the Signal over Noise Presenter Toolkit
- **Secondary CTA on /scan:** Request a Free Presentation Diagnostic

### Thank-you page
- **Primary CTA on /thanks:** Request a Free Presentation Diagnostic

## Traffic / Acquisition Context
### v1 traffic sources
- Workshop QR codes
- Direct link sharing
- Referral traffic
- General site visitors

### Conversion logic
1. QR scan lands on `/scan`
2. Visitor claims toolkit via light email capture
3. Visitor is redirected to `/thanks`
4. `/thanks` promotes the Free Presentation Diagnostic
5. Diagnostic flow leads toward consult and paid services

## Positioning / Differentiation
### Core positioning
Signal over Noise helps professionals present with more clarity, confidence, and credibility in English and multilingual settings.

### Locked homepage hero message
**Make your next presentation clearer, sharper, and more credible.**

### Working homepage subhead
**Signal over Noise helps professionals presenting in English and multilingual settings strengthen the message, refine the slides, rehearse the delivery, and prepare for high-stakes moments.**

### Differentiation direction
Signal over Noise focuses on:
- Stronger message structure
- Sharper slides
- Better rehearsal and delivery
- High-stakes presentation support
- A premium, design-aware approach that connects story, slides, and performance

### Brand distinction
The brand should feel editorial, high-contrast, institutional, and precise, not playful, soft, or startup-cute.

## Messaging Direction
### Strategic messaging rule
