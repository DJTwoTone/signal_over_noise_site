# Signal over Noise — Design Update Plan, Copy Updates, and Remaining Work

## Purpose
This document captures the design-direction decisions that are now locked, the specific page and copy updates to make next, the placeholder tracker to move into a spreadsheet, and the remaining work required to replace placeholders and ship a stronger v1.

---

# 1. Locked decisions

## Messaging
- **Locked homepage hero headline:** **Make your next presentation clearer, sharper, and more credible.**
- Use **your** instead of **the** when the line is directly about the visitor’s presentation.
- Keep the audience framing respectful and practical:
  - **Professionals presenting in English and multilingual settings**
  - **Experts and teams presenting across international, multilingual, or mixed-language contexts**
- Avoid framing the audience as deficient, weak in English, or “in need of fixing.”

## Design direction
- Keep the premium editorial / civic / high-contrast foundation.
- Add **selective editorial drama**, not clutter.
- Keep the site restrained overall, but increase tension and pull through:
  - a stronger hero
  - more varied section rhythm
  - fewer same-looking cards
  - more concrete proof artifacts
  - more distinct CTA wording by page
- Use subtle gold more intentionally for:
  - eyebrow labels
  - step numbers
  - annotations
  - small accent rules

## Trust strategy
- Lead proof with **artifacts and process**, not generic claims.
- Proof order:
  1. Sample diagnostic markup
  2. Before/after slide comparison
  3. Founder credibility
  4. Process clarity
  5. Logos / institutions only where approved

## Founder tone
- Founder section tone: **credible, concise, human**.
- No inflated founder mythology.
- No long founder essays.
- Keep founders visible as trust anchors, but let the brand lead.
