# Builder_Spec_v2

## Build Overview
Build a mobile-first, premium editorial multi-page marketing site for Signal over Noise inside Pencil. The site supports two connected funnels:

1. **Workshop QR funnel**
   - `/scan` captures leads via the Signal over Noise Presenter Toolkit
   - `/thanks` promotes the Free Presentation Diagnostic

2. **General site funnel**
   - Homepage and core pages promote the Free Presentation Diagnostic first
   - Toolkit remains a secondary lead-capture path

The build should launch quickly, allow temporary placeholders where necessary, and stay easy for Ben to update.

## Current Execution Direction
### Design behavior
- Restrained overall
- Selective editorial drama
- Fewer equal-energy cards
- Stronger proof artifacts
- One strong right-side hero module on homepage
- Gold used sparingly as attention signal only

### Locked homepage messaging
**Headline:** Make your next presentation clearer, sharper, and more credible.

**Subhead:** Signal over Noise helps professionals presenting in English and multilingual settings strengthen the message, refine the slides, rehearse the delivery, and prepare for high-stakes moments.

### 5. Process `/process`
#### Primary job
Reduce uncertainty by showing a structured, professional method.

#### Required sections
1. Process overview
2. Step 1: Start with the diagnostic
3. Step 2: Get the recommendation
4. Step 3: Refine and rehearse
5. Step 4: Present with more confidence
6. CTA

#### Locked step copy direction
- **Start with the diagnostic:** Review the current presentation, identify what is getting in the way, and clarify the highest-leverage next step.
- **Get the recommendation:** Choose the right level of support: refinement, rehearsal, sprint, coaching, or no further help if that is the honest answer.
- **Refine and rehearse:** Improve the message, sharpen the slides, and practice the delivery where needed.
- **Present with more confidence:** Move into the presentation with stronger structure, clearer visuals, and a more credible delivery.

#### Notes
Do not reuse generic filler copy under multiple steps. Add visual sequencing with spacing, numbering, and rhythm.
